**Vincent van Gogh**


**Famous Works:** Starry Night, Sunflowers, The Bedroom


Vincent van Gogh was a Dutch post-impressionist painter whose work had a profound and lasting impact on the art world despite his short and tumultuous life. Known for his bold, dramatic brush strokes, vibrant colors, and emotional intensity, Van Gogh's paintings convey deep personal expression and a unique perspective on the world around him. Although he struggled with mental illness and lived in relative obscurity during his lifetime, selling only a few paintings, Van Gogh's art gained immense recognition posthumously. His innovative techniques and poignant exploration of human experience have cemented him as one of the most influential and beloved artists in history.
