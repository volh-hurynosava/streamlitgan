**Paul Cezanne**

**Famous Works:** The Basket of Apples, Mont Sainte-Victoire
Paul Cezanne was a groundbreaking French artist whose work laid the foundation for the transition from 19th-century Impressionism to 20th-century Cubism. Known for his distinctive approach to form, color, and composition, Cézanne sought to capture the underlying structure of the natural world. His method of building form with color and his exploration of geometric simplification profoundly influenced the direction of modern art. Cézanne's innovative techniques and visionary approach earned him recognition as a key figure in the development of modern art, inspiring future artists such as Picasso and Matisse.
